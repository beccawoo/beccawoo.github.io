I am a UX designer and technical writer in the Bay Area of California, focused on empathetic design through education.

I am currently most excited to learn more about accessible tools, interaction design, and how we can continue to improve to ensure that every person is seen.

My main goal for my career is to improve the lives of others by designing for accessibility, whether that is through a project that I am adding accessibility to or a project entirely focused on it. There are so many people excluded that need empathetic advocacy, and I aspire to be one of those voices.
