Form, Composition, and Color Microsite, UX Designer

ASU Master's Program, Sept 2024

A site to showcase 12 subjective icons and variations and how they were created.
  • Composed icons using 12 shapes created prior, and created variations based on color palette and stroke size.
  • Included a development section within the microsite to detail the project and how the elements culminated together to create the full microsite.
  • Taught peers how to use Figma (tips and tricks for efficiency, prototyping) and troubleshooting syncing Adobe fonts to Figma.
