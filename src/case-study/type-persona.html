Type Persona Mobile App, UX Designer

ASU Master's Program, Oct 2024

A mobile app that highlights the characteristics of the font Neue Haas Grotesk through its simple design.
  • Distinguished 5 different font weights to test with 51 unique characters and symbols.
  • Created an interactive form field that allows the user to test the font weights, within the limitations of Figma.
  • Included a development section within the app discussing the details of the project and a guide to using the form field due to the limitations of Figma.
