Genre Analysis


ASU Masters Program, Sept 2024


A research paper analyzing the technical communication genre of proposals.
  • Researched proposals: the origins of the word, how it was used versus how it is used now, the purpose, the types, and the structure.
  • Analyzed the writer/audience relationship, the communities that use proposals, and how they fulfill their function.
  • Discusses current trends of proposals and what that says about the communities that use it and its impact on society.
