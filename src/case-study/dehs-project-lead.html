Project Lead, Design Explorers High School Internship

BRIDGEGOOD, Jun-Jul 2024

BRIDGEGOOD is a non-profit organization driven to bridge the gaps for students and career pivoters. Spent 5 weeks to teach high school students about user experience.
  • Designed a 5-week user experience curriculum with meticulous documentation for future Project Leads to use for reference and guidance.
  • Taught industry tools such as Figma as well as deliverables such as case studies, creating a passion for user experience in the high school students.
  • Introduced students to sketchnotes, empathetic feedback, and personal branding on social media (according to their personal interests) which will be useful in their future careers.
